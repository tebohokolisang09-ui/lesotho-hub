About Lesotho Farming Hub

Empowering farmers, sustaining communities, and growing the future of agriculture in Lesotho.

Who We Are

Lesotho Farming Hub is a community-driven initiative that supports farmers with knowledge, resources, and opportunities. Our aim is to transform agriculture into a sustainable and profitable sector that not only feeds our nation but also creates opportunities for growth and innovation.

Founded in 2015, we've grown to become Lesotho's premier agricultural support organization, serving over 5,000 farmers across all 10 districts. Our team of agronomists, business experts, and community developers work together to create holistic solutions for modern farming challenges.

Our Mission

To empower local farmers with modern techniques, training, and support that ensure higher yields and better livelihoods.

Our Vision

To create a thriving agricultural community where farming is innovative, sustainable, and a source of pride for Lesotho.

Our Core Values

Community
